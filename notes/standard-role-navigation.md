# Standard Role — Visible Navigation

This documents the menus and navigation items visible to a user with the **standard role**.

## Main Navigation (left sidebar)

- Calendar
- Services
- Connect
- Settings

## Settings — Sub Navigation

- Your brand
- Your profile
- Services

### MANAGE

- **Notifications** (expandable)
  - Your notifications

### MORE

- Download apps
- Refer a friend
- Log out

## Notes

- Standard role users do **not** have access to business-level settings such as:
  - Booking preferences
  - Brand customization
  - Team/customer notification settings
  - General settings
- They only see their own profile, their assigned services, and personal notifications.
