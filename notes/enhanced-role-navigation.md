# Enhanced Role — Visible Navigation

The enhanced role has the same navigation as the [standard role](./standard-role-navigation.md) with the following additional capabilities.

## Additional Capabilities

### Calendar
- Can view and manage **all team members' calendars** (not just their own)
- Can **create bookings on behalf of other team members**

### Customers
- Full **CRUD access to customer profiles**
  - Create new customer profiles
  - View customer profiles
  - Edit customer profiles
  - Delete customer profiles

## What Stays the Same

Everything else is identical to the standard role — the same navigation items are visible and the same business-level settings remain inaccessible.
