# Admin Role — Visible Navigation

The admin role has full access identical to the **business owner role** with one exception.

## Difference from Business Owner

- The admin **cannot change their own role** (self-role editing is restricted)
- The admin **cannot change the business owner's role**
- Everything else — all settings, navigation, and capabilities — is the same as the business owner

## What's the Same as Business Owner

All navigation and capabilities are available, including:
- Full business settings (brand, booking preferences, notifications, general)
- Team management (invite, manage team members)
- Full calendar access across all team members
- Full customer profile CRUD
- Services and class management
- Connect / integrations
